Simulink by MathWorks models and simulates dynamical systems, integrating real-time data for control systems and physical modeling, like an automotive control system. Ansys Twin Builder creates simulation-based digital twins in aerospace and automotive, such as an aircraft engine for predictive maintenance. Siemens MindSphere connects machines to the digital world for process optimization and maintenance, exemplified by a manufacturing plant twin. GE Predix provides real-time analytics and asset management in energy and healthcare, like a power grid twin. PTC ThingWorx integrates analytics for manufacturing, such as a production line twin. IBM Maximo uses IoT and AI for asset management in utilities, creating twins for predictive maintenance, like a utility network.
