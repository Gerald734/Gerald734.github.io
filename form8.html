Monitoring includes real-time tracking of system parameters and fault detection. Simulation allows for virtual testing and scenario analysis to improve efficiency and reduce risks. Prediction uses data to forecast future states and maintenance needs with machine learning models. Control involves remote management and automated adjustments to optimize performance. Lifecycle Management oversees the entire lifecycle of a product or system, aiding in development and improvement. These functions provide in-depth analysis and control, leading to greater efficiency, lower costs, and improved performance.
